Digital Conference Fair 2025


Empowering Communities, Digitally.

About

The Digital Community Fair is a two-day event designed to foster an inclusive platform where communities can learn about existing digital tools, get onboarded onto relevant platforms, and engage in exciting activities that showcase the value of digital technology in everyday life.
Held in Kano, Jigawa, and Katsina, the fair aims to demystify digital platforms and tools by making them accessible, fun, and relevant to everyday life and work, especially for women, youth, small businesses, and professionals.
The fair will blend exhibitions, hands-on workshops, live demos, business clinics, and gamified experiences, to create a festival-like atmosphere while addressing serious economic and technological development needs.
